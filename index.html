Artificially constructed plasmids are crucial tools
in research labs and the biotech industry,
enabling the cloning, amplification, and
expression of specific genes. However, the current
range of plasmid copy numbers is limited, which
restricts the ability to fine-tune gene expression
levels.
Motivation
•Our team is developing a unique approach to
address this limitation by using cutting-edge
computational modeling techniques and
bioinformatics tools, combined with traditional
wet lab experiments, to enable fine-tuning of
plasmid copy number.
•By increasing the variability of plasmid copy
numbers, our approach has the potential to
revolutionize the use of plasmids in synthetic
biology, opening new opportunities for
optimizing gene expression and accelerating
scientific breakthroughs.
